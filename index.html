Privacy Policy for Monet Launcher

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Controller: Nico Klein
Contact: contact@klevico.com

1. Data Collection

Monet does not collect personal data and does not create user accounts. The app contains no analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

2. Location (optional)

If the user enables the weather display and grants the location permission, the approximate location (latitude and longitude) is transmitted via HTTPS to the weather service open-meteo.com in order to retrieve local weather data. Monet does not store this information. The permission can be revoked at any time in the Android system settings.

Open-Meteo privacy terms: https://open-meteo.com/en/terms

3. In-App Purchases

Premium features are unlocked through Google Play Billing. Payment processing is handled exclusively by Google. Monet does not receive any payment information.

4. Content from Other Apps

Monet displays posters and titles from TV apps installed on the device (Continue Watching, Live Channels) using the Android TvContract API. The associated images are loaded directly from the respective providers; Monet does not transmit any user data in the process.

5. Changes

Updates to this policy will be published at the same URL.
